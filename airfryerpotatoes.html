Air Fryer Potatoes

Description

Potatoes contain a lot of starch. The starch is great for foods like mashed potatoes but makes it hard for your fries to become crispy. Soaking them in water allows the starch to come off the potato, allowing for more crispy air fried french fries. You can soak your potatoes for an hour prior to cooking.

Ingredients

The following ingredients are used:

  • Onion
  • Pesto
  • Olive oil
  • Pasta
  • Cheese
  • Salt
